Welcome to **Embedded 101** — a beginner-friendly journey from computing fundamentals to booting your own CPU and printing "Hello, World!" on a UART.

This course is based on the book *C Ninja, in Pyjama* and covers four parts:

1. **Mental Models** — History of computing, CPU model, RISC-V instruction set, lab setup
2. **Assembly Programming** — From text to binary, assembly language, directives, build process
3. **C Language** — Pointers, structs, control flow, and memory-mapped I/O
4. **Embedded Systems Programming** — Mixing asm and C, boot process, linker scripts, UART
